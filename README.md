# 📱 PhonePe Digital Payment Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of PhonePe's digital payment ecosystem by tracking transaction volume, transaction value, customer activity, payment success rates, and user behavior. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor operational performance, identify usage trends, and gain valuable business insights through interactive visualizations.

---

# 🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this dashboard is to monitor digital payment performance, evaluate customer engagement, identify high-value services, and analyze transaction trends to support business decision-making.

# 💡 Key Business Insights

- Processed **288K+ digital transactions**.
- Generated **₹3.33 Billion** in total transaction value.
- Achieved an impressive **100% payment success rate** for successful transactions.
- Recorded over **108K unique users**.
- Loan services contributed the highest transaction value among all services.
- Weekday transactions accounted for nearly **72%** of total payment activity.
- Millennials and Gen X users represented the largest customer segments.
- Month-over-Month growth remained consistently positive throughout the reporting period.
